About us

Phosphorus chemistry is at the heart of everything we do.

With a legacy of over 130 years’ experience, we are committed to providing the world’s growing population with nutritious and high-quality food and improve daily life by delivering innovative phosphate and fluorine solutions to our customers in numerous sectors ranging from pet food to the chemical, construction, horticulture, personal & health care industries.

Determined to build a sustainable future, we are developing more efficient processes, protecting resources and fostering a circular economy.

At Prayon, we set the tone in the phosphate world.

Sustainability

Shaping a sustainable future

We are pioneers in the art of designing sustainable production processes in line with circularity, achieving excellent yields with our raw materials as well as valorizing our by-products.

Our wet process, that uses up to 7 times less CO2 than the thermal process, is the world reference in phosphoric acid production.

Our ultimate goal: closing the loop through phosphate recycling.
